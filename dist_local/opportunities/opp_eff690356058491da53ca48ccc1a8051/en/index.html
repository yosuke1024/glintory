## Problem Statement **Priority Problem Addressed:** Priority Problem #2 — New players are intimidated by tiny-part complexity, causing early drop-off. Watch restoration relies heavily on visual discrimination of small, similarly-shaped metal parts often differentiated only by color tinting, surface finish, or engraved markings. Players with colorblindness, low vision, or reduced fine-motor precision face structural barriers that have nothing to do with skill or interest.
